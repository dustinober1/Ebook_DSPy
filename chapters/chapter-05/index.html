Why RAG Matters

‚ùå LMs Without Retrieval

# LM only knows training data
result = lm("What happened yesterday?")
# "I don't have access to 
#  current events..."

result = lm("What's in our docs?")
# Can't access your data!
Limited to training cutoff No private data access Prone to hallucination

‚úÖ LMs With Retrieval

# Retrieve relevant context first
docs = retriever("What happened?")
result = lm(context=docs, 
            question="What happened?")
# Accurate, grounded answer!
Access to current info Uses your data Grounded in facts
