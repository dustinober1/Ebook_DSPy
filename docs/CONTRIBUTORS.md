## How to Add Your Name

Contributors are added automatically through pull requests. When you contribute:

1. Your GitHub username will appear in the contributor list
2. Your contribution type will be noted (code, content, review, etc.)
3. Your name may appear in release notes for significant contributions

## Recognition Levels

- **Major Contributor**: 5+ significant PRs or feature contributions
- **Regular Contributor**: 3-4 PRs or consistent improvements
- **Contributor**: 1-2 PRs or helpful contributions
- **Supporter**: Reported issues, suggested improvements, or helped others
