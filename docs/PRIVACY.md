# Privacy Policy for DSPy Ebook

**Last Updated: December 13, 2025**

## Overview
This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit the DSPy ebook website or use our digital products.

## What We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Analytics Data**: Page views, time spent on pages, chapters read, and interactions
- **Technical Information**: Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Usage Patterns**: How you navigate through the ebook, which chapters you read
- **Download Information**: Which formats you download (PDF, etc.)

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information (name, email, IP address)
- Location data
- Personal files or content you create
- Sensitive personal information

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Improve the Ebook**: Understand which chapters are most helpful and where readers struggle
2. **Enhance User Experience**: Optimize the reading interface and navigation
3. **Anonymous Statistics**: Track overall usage patterns without identifying individuals
4. **Technical Optimization**: Identify and fix performance issues

## Analytics and Tracking

### Google Analytics
We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. All data is:
- **Anonymized**: IP addresses are masked
- **Aggregated**: Data is analyzed in aggregate form only
- **Optional**: You can opt out of all tracking

### Custom Tracking
We track specific ebook interactions:
- Chapter progress
- Code example views
- Download attempts
- Search usage

This data helps us understand how readers use the ebook and where we can improve.

## Your Choices

### Opting Out
You can:
1. **Reject Analytics**: Click "Reject" on the consent banner
2. **Use Privacy Mode**: Enable private browsing in your browser
3. **Browser Extensions**: Use ad/tracking blockers
4. **Download PDF**: Use the offline PDF version which has no tracking

### Data Portability
- All our data is anonymous and cannot be linked to you personally
- No data is sold or shared with third parties

## Data Storage and Security

- **Storage**: Analytics data is stored securely by Google Analytics
- **Retention**: Data is retained for 26 months (Google Analytics standard)
- **Security**: We use industry-standard security practices
- **No Personal Data**: We never collect or store personal information

## Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:
- Analytics tracking (optional, with your consent)
- Remembering your preferences (theme, progress)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## Children's Privacy
The DSPy ebook is intended for adults and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## International Users
Our services are hosted in the United States. By using our site, you agree to this data transfer.

## Compliance
We comply with:
- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- Other applicable privacy laws

### Your Rights Under GDPR
- **Right to Access**: You can request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification**: You can correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure**: You can request deletion of your data
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: You can limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability**: You can request your data in a machine-readable format
- **Right to Object**: You can object to certain processing

Since we only collect anonymous analytics data, most GDPR rights don't apply, but you can always opt out of all tracking.

### Your Rights Under CCPA
- **Right to Know**: What personal information we collect
- **Right to Delete**: Your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Sale of personal information

We don't sell personal information and only collect anonymous analytics.
